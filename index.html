Canvas serves a 22 course menu highlighting top quality and seasonal Thai ingredients, creatively prepared with our unique sense of artistry.

Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the best Thai ingredients at each time of the year. We work closely with farmers and suppliers to source ingredients of top quality from all regions of Thailand, many of which are organically grown, others are wild, others carefully selected from local markets each morning.

We don't fit into a category of Thai or Western, but we’re influenced by many international cuisines, local street food and markets, traditional Thai preparations, modern techniques, and the city of Bangkok itself. The incredible abundance of Thai ingredients drives our cooking and we share that with our guests in a creative way. Our goal is to offer an amazing dining experience that could only be had in Bangkok: bold, colorful, stimulating, and progressive.
