Canvas serves a 22 course menu highlighting top quality and seasonal Thai ingredients, creatively prepared with our unique sense of artistry.

Our menu changes seasonally to reflect the best Thai ingredients at each time of the year. We work closely with farmers and suppliers to source ingredients of top quality from all regions of Thailand, many of which are organically grown, others are wild, others carefully selected from local markets each morning.
